🌐 Welcome to The Mesh
A live crisis‑simulation game where every decision reshapes the network.
You’re about to enter a system under stress — a corporate‑university network facing cascading crises.
Your team represents a node in this network. Every choice you make changes:

Resources — your capacity to act

Trust — your credibility and reliability

Influence pathways — how strongly your decisions affect others

Your goal is simple:
Collaborate across disciplines to stabilize the system before the crisis overwhelms it.

🎮 How the Game Works
Each turn follows a simple rhythm:

A crisis event appears.  
The narrator describes what’s happening right now.

You receive three choices.  
Each one is a real organizational action — forming coalitions, deploying patches, negotiating, forecasting, etc.

Your team discusses and selects ONE action.  
Your decision updates the network graph.

The crisis evolves.  
Trust, resources, and influence shift.
New events emerge based on what you did.

📊 Your Stats
Resource Capacity — time, money, energy, operational bandwidth

Trust — your reliability and social capital

Influence Weight — how strongly your actions affect others

Small changes can cascade across the entire system.

🏆 How to Succeed
You win by stabilizing the network:

Trust is high

Resources are balanced

Influence pathways are strong

Crisis severity stops escalating

You lose when the system collapses under pressure.

💡 Tips for New Players
Talk early, talk often — silence kills networks

Share knowledge — cross‑disciplinary insight is your superpower

Build trust intentionally — it’s the most valuable stat

Don’t hoard resources — scarcity is solved through coordination

Expect cascading effects — every choice matters
