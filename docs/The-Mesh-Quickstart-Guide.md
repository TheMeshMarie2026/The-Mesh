⚡ The Mesh Quickstart Guide
Learn the game in 2 minutes. Start playing in 10.
1. What The Mesh is
The Mesh is a crisis‑simulation game where teams represent nodes in a living network.
Every decision you make changes:

Resources (your capacity to act)

Trust (your credibility in the network)

Influence pathways (how strongly your actions affect others)

Your goal:
Collaborate across disciplines to stabilize the system before the crisis overwhelms it.

2. How a Turn Works
Each turn follows the same rhythm:

1. The Narrator presents a crisis event.
A short, vivid description of what’s happening right now.

2. You receive three choices.
Each one is a real organizational action — forming a coalition, running a hackathon, deploying a patch, negotiating with another team, etc.

3. Your team discusses and selects ONE action.
Your decision updates the network graph.

4. The crisis evolves.
Your choice affects trust, resources, and influence.
The next turn’s crisis will reflect what you did.

3. What Your Stats Mean
Resource Capacity
Your ability to act — time, money, energy, operational bandwidth.

Trust
Your reliability and credibility.
High trust = more influence and better outcomes.

Influence Weight
How strongly your decisions affect other teams.

Small changes in these stats can cascade across the entire network.

4. The Actions You Can Take
You’ll see actions from several categories:

Coalitions & Coordination

Resource Management

Trust & Reputation

Governance & Policy

Information & Intelligence

Conflict Resolution

ESG & Ethics

Technical Infrastructure

Meta‑Game (Forecasting, Merging)

Classroom/Team Negotiation

Each action shifts the network in different ways.
There are no “right” answers — only consequences.

5. How to Win
You win by stabilizing the network:

Trust is high

Resources are balanced

Influence pathways are strong

Crisis severity stops escalating

You lose when:

Trust collapses

Resources deplete

Influence pathways break

Crisis severity spirals out of control

The Mesh rewards collaboration, not hierarchy.

6. Team Strategy Tips
Talk early, talk often. Silence kills networks.

Share knowledge. Cross‑disciplinary insight is your superpower.

Build trust intentionally. It’s the most valuable stat in the game.

Don’t hoard resources. Scarcity is solved through coordination.

Watch the network. Your node is never isolated.

Expect cascading effects. Every choice matters more than you think.

7. What to Do Right Now
Form your team.

Read the first crisis event.

Discuss your three choices.

Pick one.

Watch the network react.

Repeat until stability or collapse.

Welcome to The Mesh.
Let’s see how your system survives.
